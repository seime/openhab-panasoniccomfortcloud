Comments on Panasonic Comfort Cloud for Android

★★★★★ The latest update (19.06.2024) made the energy graphs better and data can be viewed easily. Made the whole experience much better.
★★★★★ Apps work good, 4 star because it doesn't support Edge browser on Phone For those have same situation like me, just download another browser (Google?) Then sign in then switch back to your favorite browser
★★★★★ The app has some nice features but with limitations. I can create schedule to turn on/off app in the night, but it isn't allowed to select air flow direction =(Internal unit makes bzzzz noise when wings change direction. I'd prefer to set static position in the night to reduce noise
★★★★★ LOVE HOW I CAN CONTROL IT FROM MY PHONE & TURN IT ON MY WAY HOME AND THE HOUSE IS NICE AND ARMIES WHEN WE GET THERE
★★☆☆☆ The app doesn't allow you to set the heating temperature before 18 deg even though the fixed goes to 16. Our indoor temperature sensor for our whole-house heat pump is several degrees colder than the bedrooms so that means we are stuck with 22 degrees in the bedroom. Also, there is no logic capability in the app, so every night, I, me, a human has to use my brain and think if it's going to be a cold night or if I'll be right. Buy Mitsubishi instead! My last heat pump and app were better.
★★☆☆☆ Badly designed, awkward to use. Requires internet even when local What makes it worse, and prevents me buying or recommending Panasonic products in the future, is lacking integration with Home Assistant and other widely used home automation tools. I can somewhat excuse the lack of Home Assistant support, but after the community go to the trouble of implementing the integration so that Panasonic doesn't have to, Panasonic repeatedly make changes that break support with zero consideration to users.
★★☆☆☆ Can't register the device after entering the password
